    Bubbles & Smile

    THE AFTER SCHOOL CLUB BY ANYTIME CHILDCARE

    Established in 2017
    Anytimechildcare Offers free Maths and English Assessments for 5-11 year olds

    Bubbles & Smile is an after/ out of school club providing normal club activities and in addition offer sports as well as provide numeracy and literacy support to our club members. Our experiences and dynamics, are drawn from our long serving staff who plan exciting, fun, themed and challenging activities, enabling children to make good progress in their development and learning, these include outdoor play, group play, arts and crafts, messy play, floor and table activities etc. it is worth noting that all these combined activities improves inter-personal skills.
